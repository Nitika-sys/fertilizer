About AgriWise

EMPOWERING FARMERS THROUGH TECHNOLOGY

AgriWise is a revolutionary platform dedicated to transforming agricultural practices through intelligent fertilizer recommendations. Our mission is to empower farmers with data-driven insights that optimize crop yields while promoting sustainable farming practices. We understand that choosing the right fertilizer is crucial for maximizing productivity and minimizing environmental impact. That's why we leverage cutting-edge technology, including AI and machine learning, to analyze various factors such as soil type, crop requirements, climate data, and historical performance to provide precise and actionable recommendations.

Our platform is designed to be user-friendly and accessible to all farmers, regardless of their technical expertise. We aim to simplify the complex process of fertilizer management, helping farmers save time, reduce costs, and improve the health and yield of their crops. Beyond just recommendations, AgriWise is building a community where farmers can share knowledge, learn best practices, and stay updated on the latest advancements in agricultural technology. We are committed to supporting the agricultural community and contributing to a more sustainable and prosperous future for farming.

Meet Our Founder

Nitika Singh

Born on August 6, 2004, in Buxar, Bihar, Nitika Singh is the visionary behind AgriWise. Currently pursuing her Bachelor's degree in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Nitika combines her technical expertise with a deep understanding of agricultural challenges. Her passion for technology and commitment to improving farming practices led to the creation of AgriWise, a platform that bridges the gap between modern technology and traditional farming methods. Through her leadership, AgriWise continues to evolve as a trusted resource for farmers seeking to optimize their agricultural practices through data-driven insights.

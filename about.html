About AgriWise

EMPOWERING FARMERS THROUGH TECHNOLOGY

AgriWise is a revolutionary platform dedicated to transforming agricultural practices through intelligent fertilizer recommendations. Our mission is to empower farmers with data-driven insights that optimize crop yields while promoting sustainable farming practices. By leveraging cutting-edge technology and agricultural expertise, we provide personalized fertilizer recommendations tailored to specific crops, soil conditions, and regional requirements.

Our platform combines traditional farming wisdom with modern technology to create a comprehensive solution for farmers. We understand the challenges faced by the agricultural community and are committed to providing accessible, accurate, and practical recommendations that help farmers make informed decisions about their fertilizer usage. Through our innovative approach, we aim to contribute to food security and sustainable agriculture practices across the nation.

Meet Our Founder

Nitika Singh

Born on August 6, 2003, in Buxar, Bihar, Nitika Singh is the visionary behind AgriWise. Currently pursuing her Bachelor's degree in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Nitika combines her technical expertise with a deep understanding of agricultural challenges. Her passion for technology and commitment to improving farming practices led to the creation of AgriWise, a platform that bridges the gap between modern technology and traditional farming methods. Through her leadership, AgriWise continues to evolve as a trusted resource for farmers seeking to optimize their agricultural practices through data-driven insights.
